World Standards Day
celebrated internationally each year on 14 October honoring the efforts of experts who develop voluntary standards within standards development organizations such as the <abbr title="International Electrotechnical Commission">iec</abbr>, <abbr title="International Organization for Standardization">iso</abbr>, and the <abbr title="International Telecommunication Union">itu</abbr>. The focal point of <strong>World Standards Day</strong> is to raise awareness among regulators, industry, and consumers as to the importance of <strong>standardization</strong> to the global economy.</p>
